02 — About

One person, three projects,
and an unreasonable number of half-finished ones.

I build small, opinionated tools for myself, then leave them online in case anyone else wants them. Most are vibe-coded with AI — written in long, loose sessions where I describe what I want and refine until it feels right.

I'm interested in software that respects your data, runs without a server when it can, and tells you the truth even when the answer isn't fun. Calm interfaces, honest defaults, and weekend-sized ambitions.

If something here is useful to you, that's the whole point. If you'd like to work on something together, my inbox is open.
